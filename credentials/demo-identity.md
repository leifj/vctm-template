# Demo Identity Credential

A demonstration verifiable credential for identity verification. This credential can be used as a template for creating your own VCTM credentials.

## Claims

- `given_name` (string): The given name of the credential holder [mandatory]
- `family_name` (string): The family name of the credential holder [mandatory]
- `birth_date` (date): Date of birth of the holder [sd=always]
- `email` (string): Email address of the holder
- `nationality` (string): Nationality of the credential holder
- `national_id` (string): National identification number [sd=always]
